About Eden Club

There are many words that one would associate with Boisar but fun was never one of them. Well, that’s about to change. The Eden Club, Boisar’s first luxury club is all set to offer a select few the lifestyle they have been longing for. Set inside Mumbai’s first truly sustainable city, Eco Eden City, the club will boast of some of the finest recreational facilities seen in this part of the world. Apart from offering members a chance to spend quality time with family, The Eden Club will also be the perfect place to relax and unwind.

About Mayuresh Group

At Mayuresh Group, everything we do is with a long term vision. Be it the quality of our construction or the effects on the environment, we go beyond looking at short term gains. We have built a solid reputation for ourselves in the last 3 decades based on these values.

Our mission is to make ‘sustainable development’, or in other words homes that harness the power of Mother Nature, like solar and wind energy, affordable for everyone. Most people have wondered how this can be done but we believe that sustainability is not expensive, it just needs time and planning.
